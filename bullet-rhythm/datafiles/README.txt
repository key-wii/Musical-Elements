The minimum viable product for The Musical Elements: A game where bosses attack to the tune of the music.

Exploit your enemy's elemental weakness and use their own power against them!

        Controls
Shoot -------------------------------------- Click Mouse
Focus Shoot (less recoil but less DPS) ----- Hold Mouse, then release
Movement ----------------------------------- Press WASD
Focus Movement (slow, precise) ------------- Hold Space/Shift + WASD

Change Crosshair --------------------------- Scroll Mouse Wheel
Laser Sight Toggle ------------------------- Press L
